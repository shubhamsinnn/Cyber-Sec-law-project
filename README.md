Academic project for **Cybersecurity Law & Policy** — a complete draft Cybersecurity Law for the fictional nation of **VIT's**.

## Overview

This project designs national cybersecurity legislation covering digital rights, cyber offenses, data protection, corporate and government responsibilities, incident reporting, the National Cyber Security Authority (NCSA), AI/emerging tech regulations, critical infrastructure, awareness programs, international cooperation, and case studies.

## Contents

The full report (`Cyberland_Cybersecurity_Law_Project.docx`) includes 14 parts:

1. Introduction & scenario context  
2. Cyber law definitions (20+ terms)  
3. Citizens' Digital Rights  
4. Cyber offenses & penalties  
5. Data protection framework  
6. Corporate responsibilities  
7. Government responsibilities  
8. Cyber incident reporting framework  
9. NCSA structure & powers  
10. AI & emerging technology regulations  
11. Critical infrastructure protection  
12. Awareness & education campaign  
13. International cooperation  
14. Fictional case studies  

## Deliverables

- Written report (included)  
- Presentation, flowcharts, and infographics (as per assignment requirements)

## Context

VIT's is modeled as a digital nation with 50M internet users facing data breaches, ransomware, banking fraud, misinformation, identity theft, and cyberbullying — addressed through this proposed legal framework.
